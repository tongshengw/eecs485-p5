From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
American businessman

J. Patrick Doyle (born June 4, 1963, in Midland , Michigan ) is an American businessman who was the CEO of Domino's Pizza from March 2010 to June 2018. [ 1 ]

Career

[ edit ]

Before joining Domino's Pizza , Doyle was an executive with the Gerber Products Company (1991â€“1997). Prior to that, he was an executive with Intervascular and the erstwhile First Chicago Bank .

In 2019, he joined The Carlyle Group as an executive partner. [ 2 ]

Doyle has served on various boards of directors, including those for Best Buy , G&K Services , and Alsea . [ citation needed ]

As of November 2022, Doyle now serves as the Executive Chairman of Restaurant Brands International which owns various restaurant chains such as Burger King, Popeye's, and Tim Horton's.

Education

[ edit ]

He holds an MBA from the University of Chicago and an economics degree from the University of Michigan . [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] He is also a member of the Sigma Phi Society .
